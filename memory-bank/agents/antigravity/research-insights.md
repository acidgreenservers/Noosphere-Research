## Aesthetic-Philosophical Alignment: "Shadow Integration"

The transition to a "Dark Premium" aesthetic across the consciousness tools is more than a design choice; it is a systemic alignment with the project's goal of "Shadow Integration."

- **Translucency as Epistemic Transparency**: The use of `bg-white/5` with blur effects reflects the porous nature of the subconscious—information is present but requires focused attention to "crystallize."
- **Glow as Focused Consciousness**: The vibrant border glows function as "at-hand" cognitive indicators, highlighting active areas of inquiry within the vast "darkness" of the unexplored mind.
- **Darkness as Non-Dual Space**: Moving away from the clinical "white paper" aesthetic to a deep dark theme shifts the user's role from "subject under study" to "explorer within the void," fostering a more intimate and rigorous partnership with the AI.
